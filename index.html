Features

Human-like mouse movement

Organic paths with natural acceleration, micro-corrections, and idle drifts. Not a simple loop — genuine randomization built on real behavioral data.

Simulated typing activity

Variable-speed keystroke patterns that mirror real human typing rhythm. Looks like someone actually working — because it should.

📜

Natural scroll behavior

Document and browser scrolling that mimics reading patterns. Speed, pauses, and direction all intelligently randomized.

🔕

Completely silent mode

Zero visual indicators. No tray icon, no window, no notifications. ClownFlowAI is invisible by design — because that's the only way it works.

Custom activity profiles

Create and save profiles for different scenarios — high activity, light browsing, reading mode. Switch profiles instantly from the launcher.

Scheduled working hours

Auto-starts at the beginning of your shift, auto-stops at the end. Set it once and it runs on your schedule, not the other way around.

Full activity log

Every simulated event logged locally. See exactly what ClownFlowAI did, when it did it, and what your monitoring software saw. Full audit trail, full control.

Windows native

Built on PowerShell and WPF — no third-party runtime, no dependencies, no admin rights required. Launches instantly on any Windows 10/11 machine.

🔒

Zero data transmitted

Everything stays on your machine. No telemetry, no analytics, no network requests. ClownFlowAI has no internet connectivity. Your activity is yours.
