## FULL PRODUCT DESCRIPTION (for the PH listing page)

### What is Ca$hClowns?

Ca$hClowns is a **free mouse jiggler app for Windows** that keeps your computer from going idle, prevents your screen from locking, and maintains your active status on Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zoom, and Webex.

It runs silently in the background, requires zero installation, needs no internet connection, and collects absolutely no data. Download the executable and it just works.

### Why We Built It

Every remote worker knows the drill: you step away for five minutes, your Teams status flips to Away, and suddenly your manager thinks you vanished. Hardware mouse jigglers cost $30–$60 and take up a USB port. Other software solutions require installation, cloud accounts, or subscriptions.

We built Ca$hClowns because the solution should be **free, instant, and require nothing from you** except downloading one file.

Oh — and we gave it a clown mascot. Because why not.

### What Makes Ca$hClowns Different

- **100% free** — no trial, no premium tier, no credit card
- **Zero installation** — single portable executable, just run it
- **No internet required** — runs completely offline, no cloud, no telemetry
- **Windows 10 & 11** — fully compatible, no .NET gymnastics
- **No admin rights needed** — works on corporate-managed laptops
- **Animated clown mascot** — the only mouse jiggler that has one

### Who It's For

- Remote workers keeping Teams/Slack status active during breaks
- Work from home employees on corporate laptops with locked settings
- IT admins keeping sessions alive during long installs and transfers
- Presenters preventing screen lock during demos
- Anyone tired of re-entering their password every 5 minutes
